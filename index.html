I am Faezeh Barahouei Pasandi and welcome to my website! I'm a senior undergraduate student, and will be graduating with a BA degree in English Literature in June 2021. Once I heard “You are what you speak.” I thought to myself, this is so true, I am not only an Iranian woman but I became a part of the English world when I started to think, to act and to exchange in another language. That is when I fell in love with languages and all their forms. To speak a language is to take on a world, a culture. I love to explore the mystical world of languages through literature which opens my perspective to other people, other souls. Also, I love to explore cultures through the language of the body, dance.

To learn more about my work, please check out my talks below!
